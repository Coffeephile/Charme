Hi there,

Charme is a distributed social network with client side encryption. This means you can choose a server, where your data is stored or even set up your own, and the server can not read your messages.

Multiple Walls

You are not interested in someones private life, but like the music she or he produces?

Charme allows you to create different walls, so called collections, which can be subscribed separately. So in this case you would only subscribe to the Music Collection.

Mobility

We will release Apps for smartphones and talbets.

Technology and Design at its finest

Charme has a minimalistic design reduced on the important functions. To reduce web traffic we only load JSON Data from server, no html.

Everything you expect from a social network

Stream, Profile, Messaging and more.

Simply all you need for communication and sharing.

The Infrastructure

The Charme network consits of many independent servers and data centers. You can choose a provider where your data is stored or even set up your own server.

We use GridFS/MongoDB in combination with PHP for a super scalable and fail safe infrastructure which is capable to maintain millions of users on a single - or multiple data centers.
